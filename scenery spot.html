Yulong Mountain

Yunlong Mountain has both natural scenery and cultural landscape. There are many cultural relics on the mountain, such as the big Stone Buddha in the Northern Wei Dynasty, the cliff stone carvings in the Tang and Song dynasties, the Fanghe Pavilion, Zhaohe Pavilion, Yinhe Spring, zhangshan people's old residence in the Song Dynasty, the Xinghua Buddhist Temple in the Ming Dynasty, the Dashiyan in the Qing Dynasty, shanxi Guild Hall, boat Hall, imperial Tablet Pavilion, tablet Corridor and other historical relics and monuments. After liberation, Yunlong Mountain has been restored and newly built yousui Xuan, Dongtian Cottage, Cross-Cloud Pavilion, Yunlong Academy, Xinghua Village, viewing platform, Tongxin Platform and other historical attractions.
