A bite of China--xuzhou Cuisine

Xuzhou cuisine inherits dapeng flavor, exquisite production, unique flavor, its own system, spicy fresh sweet both, is the heaven of foodies. Xuzhou is located in the five provinces of the tongqu, the flavor of food naturally combines the strength of hundreds of people, but xuzhou people never come and do not pursue the principle of bringing food to Xuzhou, foreign food to Xuzhou can, but must conform to the eating habits and taste of Xuzhou people, all the food after localization, both retain the original flavor and fusion of Xuzhou characteristics.

Eight fried dough sticks have a history of more than 100 years in Xuzhou. There are two kinds of wheel shape and oval shape. Because the finished product is golden in color, crisp in fragrance and evenly distributed, it is called eight fried dough sticks. In 2000, it was named "Chinese famous snack". The production of eight fried dough sticks jiangsu snack. Make strips with water mass. Eight closed, fried from. Crisp and delicious, unique shape.
