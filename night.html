The Night Life In Xuzhou

After you had learn so many about Xuzhou, now I will tell you the most romantic part
--The night culture.

When every shopping mall is closed, when every lights turned on
the night life of Xuzhou has begin.

You could see that, Xuzhou is a city with hills and lakes.
When night comes, the colorful lights around lakes and hills will appear.


There is a concert by the sade of the lake. This is a long bridge connecting a few islets.

Also, the pavilions near the Smallsouthern Lake really beautiful.

The night views of Xuzhou is a huge light show.

But, that's not all of Xuzhou's night culture!

The night market is also a important part of night culture of Xuzhou.

It maybe don't so fashion as shopping mall,
but it's the most jollification part of Xuzhou.

The citizens of Xuzhou maybe the citizens who like night best.
In here, when people feel stressed, they may choose to enjoy BBQ and drink all the night, or go to the KtV to release pressure.
